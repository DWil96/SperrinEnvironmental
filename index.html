UK Engineering — Omagh, Co. Tyrone

Purpose-Built Slurry Separation
for Modern Agriculture

Sperrin Separators is a UK engineering company based in Omagh, Co. Tyrone, dedicated to delivering innovative slurry management solutions for farms across Britain and Ireland. Our flagship product, the Sperrin Optiflow, efficiently separates the solid and liquid fractions of animal slurry — improving waste handling, nutrient management, and enabling effective sand recovery. Manufactured in the UK by Weld-Tech Engineering Services Ltd.
